A — Washi & Ink (japandi / wabi-sabi)

Warm washi-paper ground, soft ink text, airy letterspaced type, hairline rules, things slightly off-grid and organic. Muted, calm, unhurried. A single vermilion seal as the only ornament. Fun comes entirely from silky motion.
